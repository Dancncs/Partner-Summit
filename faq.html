Is there a deadline to register?

Registration for the event will begin on August 22, and will close on September 26, 2017.

How will the hotel and travel costs be handled?

We have conference packages with accommodation inclusive, you may select this option should you like to take advantage of our exclusive rates negotiated for the event guests. Please provide us your flight details during registration and we will book the necessary for you.

Hotel Reservations - Cancellation Policy
Due to high occupancy, cancellations received after 5 September, 2017 will be subject to full penalty of the accommodations booked. For any amendments of stay duration, please inform the Event Secretariat at the below contact by email before the 5 September.

What are the transportation options to/from the airport and The Sheraton Saigon?

Airport transfer is provided for all our guests travelling within the event duration (Arrivals on 4 Oct / Departures on 7 Oct). The Tan Son Nhat International Airport is a 30-minute drive away from The Sheraton Saigon.

What are the check in and out times at the hotel?

How do I make changes to my registration?

You may make changes to your registration by revisiting the registration website with your email address and confirmation number (sent to you in your registration confirmation email). Do I require a travel visa to Vietnam?

A Vietnam visa is required for all nationalities except those eligible for a visa exemption. With the visa exemption program, visitors can enter Vietnam with just their passport and a return ticket. If these visitors wish to stay longer than the stated period, they can apply for a visa at a local Vietnamese Embassy or Consulate; or apply online for a visa-on-arrival.
